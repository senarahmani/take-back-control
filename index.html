Before you pay anyone — read this

Most real help is free. Here's how to spot a scam.

Predatory "removal services" prey on people in crisis. Almost everything that actually protects you costs nothing. The big tells:

"Pay us and it'll all disappear." No one can guarantee total removal from the whole internet. Anyone promising it is selling certainty that doesn't exist.
They want you to upload the images "to find them." Legitimate tools fingerprint on your device and never take the file. Handing images to a stranger is the exact risk you're escaping.
Urgency plus a subscription. "Act now or it spreads forever" followed by a monthly "monitoring" fee. Real monitoring (StopNCII, Google's tools) is free.
When paying is reasonable: a vetted lawyer for a known perpetrator, or a reputable firm for very high-volume cases — but only after a free support org says it's needed. Start free.
